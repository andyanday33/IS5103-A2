introduction

Web accessibility is the practise of limiting the boundaries that interrupt interaction with, or access to, websites on the World Wide Web for people with various kinds of disabilities or socio-economic restrictions on bandwidth and speed.

The web is a world constructed for and by people from different countries, ages, expertise, skills, and social status. Nowadays, web users can access the web from all kinds of devices such as their personal phones, iPad, tablets, and computers. It is common that the web users may have any kind of disability due to their birth, ageing, accidents, or trauma.

Therefore, providing web accessibility is a way to ensure that a large range of users can access and use web pages.

Target Audience

The target audience of this website is beginner developers who already have a knowledge of web technology and are beginning to think about web accessibility to increase the overall performance of developing their webpages.

Common Disabilities: Intro

Three common groups of users with limited accessibility are:

Aged web viewers

Much of the web is not accessible for aged people. Every individual needs the internet to communicate with each other, to search solutions for their working tasks and to address basic information needed. 

The accessibility regulations or policies are not enough to guarantee web accessibility. Moreover, even a few websites meet the guidelines, some elder users can still encounter accessible issues.

Visually Impaired Users

Compared with the amount of regular web users without any impairments, the number of blind users seems to be less, however, it is important to raise the concern of providing accessible and usable web service to the blind. It is equal for everyone to have access to and make attributes to the development of Internet.

Users with Reduced Mobility

People with reduced mobility often encounter a lot of difficulties when accessing the web. They may not be able to move their hands or legs as flexibly as normal people. When using a computer to access the Internet, it may not be possible to complete some specific operations through the keyboard or mouse. For them, the web page is not highly accessible.
